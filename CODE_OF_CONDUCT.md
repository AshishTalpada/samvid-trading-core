# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Samvid Trading Core community will be treated with respect and dignity.

As contributors and maintainers in this community, we commit to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior Examples
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Attributing credit appropriately
- Asking questions thoughtfully
- Helping others learn and grow

### Unacceptable Behavior Examples
- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing)
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination or exclusion based on protected characteristics
- Intimidation or threats
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behaviors

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the Samvid Trading Core project, including:
- Repository (issues, PRs, discussions, commits)
- Project meetings and communications
- Community forums and chat channels
- Conferences or events representing the project
- Personal communication regarding the project

This Code of Conduct also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by:

1. **Email**: [To be configured by maintainers]
2. **Private GitHub Issue**: Contact a maintainer directly
3. **GitHub Safety Report**: Use GitHub's built-in safety features

Reports should include:
- Description of the incident
- People involved
- Context and timeline
- Evidence if available
- Your contact information
- Any remedies you seek

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and promptly.

### Investigation Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: You will receive confirmation within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: Impartial investigation of all aspects
3. **Findings**: Results communicated within 7 days
4. **Resolution**: Appropriate action taken and documented

### Confidentiality

Reporters' identities will be kept confidential except when:
- Required by law
- Necessary to protect someone from harm
- With explicit consent of the reporter

## Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

### Warning
- Private message explaining the issue
- Request for behavior change
- Documented for records

### Temporary Restriction
- Temporary ban from participation (typically 7-30 days)
- Access revoked from repository or communication channels
- Message explaining reason and appeal process

### Permanent Removal
- Permanent ban from the community
- All contributions reviewed for removal
- Clear communication of reasons

### Legal Action
- Severe violations may trigger legal proceedings
- Law enforcement involvement if necessary

## Appeal Process

If you believe enforcement action was unjustified:

1. Submit written appeal within 14 days
2. Include new information or context
3. Different reviewer will assess appeal
4. Decision communicated within 7 days

## Community Impact

We are committed to being a welcoming community. To this end:

- We value diverse perspectives and experiences
- We actively work to eliminate discrimination
- We create an environment where all feel safe contributing
- We support each other in adhering to these standards
